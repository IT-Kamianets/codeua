Brand

Documentation and examples for waw’s logo and brand usage guidelines.

Have a need for waw’s brand resources? Great! We have only a few guidelines we follow, and in turn ask you to follow as well.

Logo

When referencing waw, use our logo mark. Do not modify our logos in any way. Do not use waw’s branding for your own open or closed source projects.

Name

waw should always be referred to as just waw. No capital w.

License FAQs

Commonly asked questions about waw’s open source license.

waw is released under the MIT license and is copyright 2024. Boiled down to smaller chunks, it can be described with the following conditions.

It requires you to:

  • Keep the license and copyright notice included in waw’s CSS and JavaScript files when you use them in your works

It permits you to:

  • Freely download and use waw, in whole or in part, for personal, private, company internal, or commercial purposes
  • Use waw in packages or distributions that you create
  • Modify the source code
  • Grant a sublicense to modify and distribute waw to third parties not included in the license

It forbids you to:

  • Hold the authors and license owners liable for damages as waw is provided without warranty
  • Hold the creators or copyright holders of waw liable
  • Redistribute any piece of waw without proper attribution
  • Use any marks owned by waw in any way that might state or imply that waw endorses your distribution
  • Use any marks owned by waw in any way that might state or imply that you created the waw software in question

It does not require you to:

  • Include the source of waw itself, or of any modifications you may have made to it, in any redistribution you may assemble that includes it
  • Submit changes that you make to waw back to the waw project (though such feedback is encouraged)

The full waw license is located in the project repository for more information.
